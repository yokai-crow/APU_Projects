The Rock Climbing In Bimal Nagar

Author - Gokul Pathak
19 January. 10 min read





Some Highlights

✔A five-hour bus drive from Kathmandu will take you to this site which is located in the Kathmandu-Pokhara route.
✔Along with the climbing experience, this tour also offers climbers with splendid views of Mt. Manaslu and the Marshyandi River.
✔Five minutes away from the main road, this rock climbing site has 55 meter rock face with four pitches consisting of some single pitches.
