The Rock Climbing In Hattiban

Author - Gokul Pathak
19 January. 10 min read

Hattiban is a beautiful village about an hour outside of Kathmandu located 15 km south south of Kathmandu valley. It is about 45 minutes drive and a 20-minute hike through the jungle back to the crag where we climb. This Hattiban rock climb excursion includes 5.9 – 5.12-grade routes and is best for autumn and spring rock climbing.


Hattiban Rock climbing is a challenging form of adventure created for those who are seeking to try something daring, different and new amongst the wilderness, near to the nature and far from the busy crowd. The popularity of Rock climbing in Nepal has been increasing in a whole another level due to its challenging yet exciting nature of adventure which certainly tests your patience, dedication, endurance and physical fitness.


'Summit the naturally formed rocks in the gracious Hattiban while being surrounded with peaceful pine trees along with the company of chirping birds and the majestic view of the Pharping Valley.'


Rock climbing in Hattiban brings you one of the exciting and serene environments filled with scenic views. Despite of your tiredness and several attempts, you will still be left wanting for more because this kind of climbing adventure in Nepal surely ignites your inner adventurous spirit to a whole new level. This Rock climbing in Kathmandu introduces you to the use of several equipments of climbing which takes a broader form in mountain and ice climb. There will be the use of equipments like Climbing rope, helmet, chalk powder, harness, Belay device and many other equipments which will be explained to you by Nepal Nomad’s professional climbing guide. In order to reach our rock climbing spot in Hattiban, we will have to undertake an uphill hike for about half an hour. While on your way to the climbing point, you will be ascending through the forest filled with pines. The Rock climbing in Pharping brings you the encounter of monasteries, Buddhist monks, Buddhist schools and the generous local people living there.


The whole experience of Rock climbing in Dakshinkali gives you a new outlook on adventure. It is more like a challenge to yourself and your strength which can be attained with enough dedication and passion. The feel of wind blowing while extending towards the top of the rock will definitely refresh you and provide you with a push of motivation. We promise you that you will be feeling immense pride and joy when leaving after the whole day of rock climbing adventure in the Pharping Valley.
